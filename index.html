about me

I've always loved arts and creating things. And as with someone with deep interest in aesthetics, I also dreamed of becoming a full-fledge fine artist, or a graphic designer at least. But things doesn't always go your way, and I ended up taking Computer Science instead.

At first I found it hard to take interest in programming. It's too technical for me. But as I walk towards its world and come to know the beauty of technology, I have learned to love it.

Now, I am both equipped with basic knowledge and experience, I want to keep on learning, growing, and making things together with my love for technology and arts, seeking out new perspective to their never ending beauty, because it has always been more fun learning and doing things that you love.
