A little bit about me

As someone who loves beautiful and artistic creations, I once dreamt of becoming a fine artist. But I somehow ended up with a degree in Computer Science and wasn't able to pursue any multimedia and digital arts course.

With each year passing during my college days, I have learned to love what I'm doing, coding. It has opened up a whole new world for me and introduced me to a complicated yet beautiful world of technology.

Now that I've been equipped with knowledge, I wanted to keep on learning new things, keep on growing, and keep on creating amazing things together with my love of the arts and technology, seeking out new perspective in problem solving and designing.
